About Me

I am a senior year student of biomedical engineering in the Universidad de los Andes in Bogotá, Colombia. I'm also finishing two minor degrees, one in Computational Mathematics and one in Bioinformatics. My main interests are medical imaging, computer vision, deep learning and data science. I'm currently working as an undergraduate research assistant in the CinfonIA research center led by professor Pablo Arbeláez. My goal is to use the power of artificial inteligence to make future medicine more easier, faster and more effective.
